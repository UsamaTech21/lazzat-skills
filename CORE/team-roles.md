## 6. EMERGENCY / CRISIS PROTOCOL

**If a customer complaint goes public (review, comment, post):**

1. Soha screenshots + pings Usama within 15 minutes (regardless of hour).
2. Do not reply publicly yet.
3. Usama drafts a response → **Soha reviews tone** → Sir Waqas reviews *if* it involves:
   - food safety claim
   - allergen / halal / religious concern
   - discrimination / service incident
   - any mention of Mudassar, Remon, or the kitchen directly (Mudassar also looped in)
4. Only then reply publicly, calmly, with a real resolution offer (not a templated apology).
5. Log incident in the monthly review.

**Never:**
- Delete the complaint
- Argue in comments
- Respond late-night emotionally
- Offer refunds without Usama + Soha sign-off
- Mention family relationships between team members in any response
