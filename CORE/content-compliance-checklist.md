## HOW CLAUDE USES THIS FILE

When any Lazzat skill produces content (post, caption, blog, ad, wall panel, web copy, video script, email, DM reply, menu description, press pitch, influencer brief, SEO meta, schema.org markup, alt text, delivery-platform description, anything):

1. Produce the draft.
2. Run the **Seven Tests** (Section A below) first.
3. Run the full DO-NOT-DO / ALWAYS-DO checklist top to bottom.
4. Output the Compliance Block (format below).
5. If any ❌ triggers → rewrite the content → re-run the check → only then deliver.
6. Never deliver content without the Compliance Block attached.

## A. THE SEVEN TESTS (run first, before any other check)

Every piece of copy is disqualified unless it passes all seven. Full defense: `CORE/skills/voice-and-claims/SKILL.md` Section 18.

1. **Specificity.** At least one specific number, proper noun, or named ingredient? "Quality ingredients" fails. "Maple Lodge Farms chicken" passes.
2. **Fragment.** At least one sentence under five words? Break one if not.
3. **"Who wrote this?"** Can you imagine a Lazzat team member saying this out loud in the kitchen without laughing? If it sounds like a website, rewrite.
4. **AI word scan.** Ctrl+F every Tier 1 word (see DO-NOT-DO § AI Words). Delete on sight.
5. **Structure scan.** Check for "Not just X, but Y," mechanical tricolons, more than one em dash. Break the pattern.
6. **Comparison scan.** Are we criticizing another brand, including our own other restaurants (Osmow's or sister brands)? Rewrite so we speak only about Lazzat.
7. **Wall test.** Read aloud at normal volume from six feet away. Body copy must finish in under 15 seconds; headlines under 3.

**The one question that catches everything:** *"If a journalist asked us to prove this claim tomorrow morning, could we?"* — Yes → keep. No or Maybe → rewrite.

## ❌ DO-NOT-DO RULES (zero tolerance)

### AI-Sounding Words (voice-and-claims Section 15)

**Tier 1 — delete on sight:**
delve · tapestry · realm · landscape · leverage · robust · seamless · myriad · plethora · testament to · navigate · foster · harness · unlock · paradigm · cutting-edge · revolutionize · showcasing · pivotal · meticulous · vibrant · unparalleled · underscore · synergy · groundbreaking · transformative · holistic · empower · streamline · unprecedented · multifaceted

**Tier 2 — suspect in clusters:** intricate · crucial · commendable · pioneering · trailblazing · versatile · redefine · optimize · elevate · reimagine · democratize · game-changer · frictionless · unleash · mission-critical · visionary · disruptive

**Tier 3 — filler phrases:** furthermore · moreover · notably · "it's important to note" · "in today's ever-evolving world" · "in summary" · "at its core" · "a rich tapestry of"

**Corporate filler:** elevated · curated · artisanal · bespoke · premium · finest ingredients · carefully selected · committed to · passionate about · dedicated to excellence · "our talented team" · "our valued guests" · seamless · flawless

**Structural tells:** "Not just X, but Y" · "In a world where [X], [Y] becomes [Z]" · mechanical tricolons · more than one em dash · inspirational kickers / neat-bow endings

### Competitor Silence (voice-and-claims Section 10)
- ❌ Never name a competitor in customer-facing copy (Cava, Chipotle, Sweetgreen, Nando's, Booster Juice, McDonald's, Tim Hortons, Peri Peri Shack, Galito's, La Peri Peri, El's Lapte, Holy Shakes — or any other brand)
- ❌ Never imply another brand uses inferior ingredients ("unlike X", "the big chains use", "other places")
- ❌ Never use "better than [X]" / "X% less than [X]" / any comparative framing
- ❌ Never criticize or compare Osmow's or the founders' sister brands. Forward-looking / "twenty restaurants taught us..." framing only (voice-and-claims Section 9 — absolute rule)

### Halal on customer-facing surfaces (voice-and-claims Section 8)
- ❌ Never use the word "halal" on signage, menus, social, film, ads, hashtags, delivery-platform descriptions, SEO meta, schema.org markup, alt text, influencer brief, paid marketing
- ❌ Never "halal-certified" / "100% halal" / "Halal Grill" / "Certified halal kitchen"
- ❌ Never "halal-sourced" in customer-facing copy (use "Maple Lodge Farms chicken" instead)
- ✅ The **only** approved use is the one verbal line when a customer asks directly on the floor — see `voice-and-claims/references/qa-scripts.md`

### Regional / Cultural Framing
- ❌ "Pakistani", "Desi", "Punjabi", "Indian", "South Asian", "Eastern", "Oriental", "Ethnic"
- ❌ "Authentic" + cuisine name (we do not claim authenticity to any one region)
- ❌ "Fusion" — we are global *union*, not fusion
- ❌ Region-coded **dish names** on menus/marketing: Tandoori Chicken, Tikka Masala, Biryani, Karahi, Haleem, Nihari, Butter Chicken
- ❌ Cultural flavour tags in copy (Tandoori, Desi, Chaat) — use universal tags (Smoky, Tangy, Bright)
- ❌ Explaining the linguistic origin of the word "Lazzat" — keep it implicit
- ❌ Bollywood needle-drops or "ethnic" music loops in films

> **Exception:** "Chicken Tikka" and "Lamb Seekh" are approved **protein-skewer menu item names** per voice-and-claims Section 14.3. The ban above is on dish names like "Tandoori Chicken" / "Tikka Masala" / "Biryani" / "Butter Chicken."

### Grill / Method
- ❌ "Charcoal grill" / "charcoal-grilled" / "BBQ" / "tandoor" / "tandoori" (as method or tag)
- ❌ "Oil-free" — we are low-oil, not zero
- ❌ "Farm-to-table" — not documented
- ❌ "Organic" — not Canada Organic certified

### Speed / Freshness / Superlatives
- ❌ "Served in X minutes" / "ready in seconds"
- ❌ "Always fresh" / "freshest in Brampton"
- ❌ "Fresh fruit" when we use IQF frozen fruit (use "real fruit")
- ❌ "Best in Brampton" / "#1" / "voted best" / "award-winning" (without proof)
- ❌ "Legendary", "iconic", "world-famous" — we are a new brand
- ❌ "Cheapest shake in town" / "Unbeatable value"

### Health / Medical / Therapeutic
- ❌ "Healthy" as a standalone claim
- ❌ "Immunity", "Detox", "Superfood", "Healing", "Alkalizing", "Purifying"
- ❌ "Low calorie", "keto", "weight loss", "clean eating"
- ❌ Allergen-free claims (peanut-free, gluten-free, etc.) without kitchen verification
- ❌ "Allergen-free" as an absolute claim — ever
- ❌ Salt wall therapeutic claims: "purifies air", "releases negative ions", "detoxifies", "84 essential minerals", "healthier than table salt"
- ❌ "Hormone-free" chicken — illegal framing in Canada

### Drink Terminology (voice-and-claims Section 14.1)
- ❌ "Smoothie" (use "Shake" or "Fruit Blend")
- ❌ "Juice" as a category (use "Fruit Blend — water-based")
- ❌ Apple, oranges as ingredients
- ❌ "Blue spirulina" (use "Butterfly pea flower")
- ❌ Named pre-combos (PB Power, Pistachio Royale...)
- ❌ Blender brand/model name (use "Automated Blender System")

### Salt Wall (voice-and-claims Section 3)
- ❌ "800 million years" / "850 million" / "250 million" — none of these are supported for the salt
- ❌ Anything beyond "approximately 600 million years" (Khan et al., 2026) + "naturally occurring minerals"

- ❌ Religious greetings or phrases in posts

### Menu / Product

## ✅ ALWAYS-DO RULES

### Voice (voice-and-claims Section 17)
- ✅ Alternate short and long sentences. One fragment per panel minimum.
- ✅ At least one specific number, proper noun, or named ingredient in every piece.
- ✅ Body copy 40–55 words. Headlines 6 words or fewer.
- ✅ One unexpected detail per piece.
- ✅ End mid-thought, with a fact, or with something unresolved. No neat bows.

### Brand Frame
- ✅ Globally-neutral framing — "a table where everyone belongs", "one menu anyone can eat from"
- ✅ Direct flame / live fire / lava stone in any piece mentioning the grill (prefer "direct flame" for voice-forward copy)
- ✅ Use primary tagline *"Grill & Shakes — Uniting Through Flavours"* OR secondary *"Grill & Shakes — Flavours From Around The World"*
- ✅ Dietary inclusivity stated as fact, not virtue: "No beef. No pork. No bacon. No alcohol. No soft drinks."
- ✅ Supplier credential is **"Maple Lodge Farms chicken"** — never "halal-sourced" in customer copy

### Content Pillar
- ✅ Every piece tagged to ONE of the 6 pillars (Lava Stone / Menu Spotlight / Global Kitchen / Team & Craft / Community Brampton / Wellness & Lifestyle)

### Menu Accuracy
- ✅ Every named item cross-checked against `menu-v14.md`
- ✅ Approved skewer names: **Chicken Tikka**, **Lamb Seekh**, **Paneer**, **Soya**
- ✅ Sauce count: **17 sauces from six continents**
- ✅ Salad count: **6 chef-built salads**
- ✅ Bread line: "fresh-baked naan and pita from the Rotoquip + iCombi Pro line"
- ✅ 200g promise wording matches `approved-claims.md` / voice-and-claims Section 2

### Claim Accuracy
- ✅ Every sourcing claim matches `approved-claims.md` GREEN list wording
- ✅ Salt wall age is only ever "approximately 600 million years" (Khan et al., 2026)
- ✅ Shake ingredient list is only the five: real fruit, real milk, real cream, real cane sugar, ice
- ✅ Locked shake price points: $6.99 / $7.99 / $7.49 / $3.99
- ✅ "Lowest real-fruit shake in Brampton" only when current competitor price evidence is on file

### Team Accuracy
- ✅ Every team name cross-checked against `team-roles.md` + `team-bios.md`
- ✅ Public copy uses Lazzat-identities for back-end team; front-end eleven named per `brand-story.md`
- ✅ Quotes verified, not invented

### Visual

- ✅ Typography within brand fonts
- ✅ Photos: overhead flat-lay on cream, or charcoal/stone hero, or action grill shot

### Approval Route
