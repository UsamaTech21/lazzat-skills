## Audience 4 — Retargeting Pools (Meta + Google)

### Pool A — Website visitors (30 / 60 / 90 days)
- All pages, excluding checkout (if applicable)
- Exclude current catering inquirers (different funnel)

### Pool B — Menu page viewers (high-intent, 30 days)
- Landing on /menu OR scrolled >50% on menu page
- Show: reels highlighting menu items + "Order Now" creative

### Pool C — Catering page viewers (very high-intent, 60 days)
- Landing on /catering
- Show: catering platter creative + "Book Catering" CTA

### Pool D — Engaged Instagram followers (30 days, no conversion)
- Liked, commented, saved, or sent message in past 30 days
- Suppress from Pool A to avoid double-hitting

### Pool E — Past catering inquirers (180 days)
- Cross-sell future events: Eid, Diwali, Christmas, summer BBQ, back-to-school
- Manual upload from CRM every 2 weeks

## Language-Specific Creative Variants

Even though the creative stays global, targeting may use language signal:

| Language targeting | Creative variant |
|---|---|
| English | Standard brand creative |
| Urdu | Same creative, caption translated for Urdu-first Brampton residents (creative tone stays global; never regional) |
| Punjabi | Same creative, caption translated (same rule) |
| Hindi | Same creative, caption translated (same rule) |

**Translated creatives never run any regional framing in imagery or dish naming — menu items stay as named in `menu-v14.md`.**

## Frequency Caps

| Campaign type | Cap |
|---|---|
| Awareness (Reach) | 3 impressions per 7 days per user |
| Consideration / Traffic | 5 impressions per 7 days |
| Conversion / Leads | uncapped (let the algorithm optimize) |
| Retargeting | 7 impressions per 7 days |

---

## Do Not Target

- Age < 18 or > 65 (brand conservative)
- General "Hindu religion" interest (per Lazzat-Founder)
- "Pakistan" / "India" / "Pakistani cuisine" / "Indian cuisine" as interest terms (regional framing)
- Gambling / nightlife / alcohol-adjacent audiences
- Political affinity audiences

---

## Exclusion List (apply to every campaign)

- Past Lazzat employees (via custom list)
- Competitor-brand engagers at Very High intent (low-ROI audience)
- Under 18
- Users outside Canada / outside GTA (use location = GTA + 3 km radius rule)
