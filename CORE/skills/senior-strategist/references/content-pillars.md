# The 6 Content Pillars — In Depth

> **Source:** `CORE/brand-guidelines.md` + Lazzat Marketing Strategy doc
> **Owner:** Lazzat-Brand
> **Rule:** Every piece of Lazzat content maps to exactly ONE pillar. Cross-over is fine; primary assignment is not negotiable.

Weekly target mix: **20 / 25 / 15 / 10 / 15 / 15**.

---

## Pillar 1 — Lava Stone Story (20%)

### What it is
The craft of the grill itself. Why volcanic stone beats charcoal. The eco angle. The method.

### Angles
- The flame itself (B-roll with slow-mo sizzle)
- Side-by-side: what a lava stone does that charcoal doesn't
- Sustainability: no carbon ash, reusable stone, low-oil
- Heat science: radiant vs convective (simplified)
- Chef's hands on stone (hero action shot)

### Formats
- Reels (action + sizzle)
- Carousels (infographics, cross-sections)
- BTS stories (stone heating + cleaning)
- Long-form blog ("Why we grill on volcanic stone")

### Hook templates
- "We replaced charcoal with something better."
- "This is what lava-stone sizzle looks like in slow-mo."
- "Zero charcoal. Zero compromise. Here's how."
- "The reason our chicken tastes like chicken — not smoke cover."

### Menu items that pair naturally
- Chicken Cube, Lamb Cube, Prawn Cube (protein hero moments)
- All Kababs (seekh / chapli / shami)
- Paneer & Tofu Cube (vegetarian / vegan moments)

## Pillar 2 — Menu Spotlight (25% — highest share)

### What it is
The items themselves. Bowl-builds. Sauces. Platters. The format customers actually order.

### Angles
- One cube deep-dive per week
- One sauce deep-dive per week
- One platter story per week
- The build-a-bowl format (carousel walkthrough)
- Seasonal drops (ask Lazzat-Founder before teasing)

### Formats
- Carousels (bowl-build walkthrough)
- Reels (plating + finish)
- Flat-lay photos
- Short form: "Name the sauce" quiz
- Long form: menu-deep blog

### Hook templates
- "17 sauces. One for every story. Today: [sauce name]."
- "Your bowl, your way — here's how."
- "200 grams of protein. Honestly weighed. Every time."
- "One platter. A table of 6. Watch."

### Menu items
- Rotate through the entire menu v14 over 12–16 weeks

## Pillar 3 — Global Kitchen (15%)

### What it is
Flavour provenance told the Lazzat way — respectful, curious, non-regional.

### Angles
- Culture-of-the-week (short, factual, never appropriative)
- Sauce-origin micro-stories (tahini → MENA; tzatziki → Greek; harissa → North African)
- Ingredient-of-the-week (saffron, cumin, pomegranate)
- Cultural festival tie-ins (handled with demographics skill's sensitivity)
- "One bowl, three heritages" feature

### Formats
- Carousels (perfect for origin storytelling)
- Long-form captions
- Thread-style reels

### Hook templates
- "This sauce travelled 4,000 kilometres to reach your plate."
- "Where harissa actually comes from."
- "Every bowl has a passport."
- "Three continents. One lava stone."

### Rules
- Never claim "authentic" anything
- Always attribute origin respectfully
- Never use region as a marketing shortcut

## Pillar 4 — Team & Craft (10%)

### What it is
The people behind Lazzat. The open kitchen. The rituals. The professor validation.

### Angles
- Chef Lazzat-Culinary at the prep station
- Chef Lazzat-Standards at the lava stone
- Lazzat-Culinary validating a new sauce
- Lazzat-Founder's founder minute (rare, always pre-approved)
- Floor team portrait series (full Lazzat-Service team — 6 members)
- "A day in the open kitchen" long-form
- Prep ritual ASMR reels

### Formats
- Documentary-style reels
- Portrait carousels
- Founder long-form blog
- BTS stories

### Hook templates
- "Meet the chef who weighs every 200 grams."
- "Our professor-validated recipes — and the professor who did the validating."
- "Seven chefs, six countries, one grill."
- "The open kitchen rule: nothing hidden."

### Rules
- Consent always (see `CORE/team-bios.md` §11)
- Never invent quotes
- Never skip a name in a group shot caption

## Pillar 5 — Community Brampton (15%)

### What it is
The city. The neighbourhoods. The customers. The catering moments. The street-level brand.

### Angles
- "One table, every story" — UGC from customers (with consent)
- Brampton neighbourhood features (Castlemore, Downtown)
- Catering-in-the-wild (offices, schools, events)
- Community event tie-ins (through demographics skill)
- Open-kitchen visitors (local influencers, schools)
- Charity moments (Ramadan Iftar, Thanksgiving drive — when run)

### Formats
- Reels (customer-led)
- Carousels (neighbourhood features)
- Long-form blog (city series)

### Hook templates
- "Today's table: the [name] family in Castlemore."
- "Catering arrived. Office stopped working."
- "McVean Dr to Clarence St — two addresses, one grill."
- "Brampton made Lazzat. Lazzat feeds Brampton."

### Rules
- Consent on every customer feature (written DM reply acceptable)
- Don't play favourites — rotate neighbourhoods
- Keep charity moments under-polished and authentic

## Pillar 6 — Wellness & Lifestyle (15%)

### What it is
Dietary inclusivity, fitness-friendly bowls, shake & fruit-blend moments, gym / studio partnerships.

### Angles
- Vegan hero (Tofu Cube, Falafel Wrap)
- Vegetarian hero (Paneer Cube, Greek Salad)
- Macro-friendly bowl math (without medical claims — just honest macros)
- Shake & Fruit Blend moments (cooling, clean presentation)
- Fitness partner shout-outs (yoga studios, gyms, run clubs)
- "After the run" bowl stories

### Formats
- Reels (bowl-build + fitness partner tie-in)
- Carousels (vegan / vegetarian options overview)
- Stories (shake-of-the-day)

### Hook templates
- "Plant-forward doesn't mean plant-only."
- "After the run, before the Monday meeting."
- "The shake that earned a photograph."
- "One menu. Every diet. Every day."

### Rules
- NO medical claims ("healthy", "low-calorie", "keto", "detox")
- Macros only if kitchen has signed off on exact numbers
- Framing is "lifestyle + inclusivity", not "diet food"

---

## Pillar Mixing Rules

- **Single post:** one primary pillar, one permitted secondary angle.
- **Weekly plan:** all 6 pillars must appear across 7 posts.
- **Monthly audit:** distribution must sit within ±5% of the 20/25/15/10/15/15 target.
- **Quarterly retune:** Lazzat-Brand rebalances based on what pillar drove the most engagement + the most orders.
