### Month 1 (Apr 26 → May 26) — "Reviews first"

- 🎯 Focus: Google reviews velocity + first social momentum
- 🎯 Pillar mix: Lava Stone (30%), Menu Spotlight (30%), Team & Craft (20%), Community Brampton (20%)
- 🎯 Cadence: 5 social posts/week (bump from 4)
- 🎯 Ads: NONE. Organic only.
- 🎯 Store action: every order bag gets a QR review card + one-line ask from the server
- 🎯 First influencer visit: week 2 (not day 1)

### Month 2 (May 26 → Jun 26) — "Delivery goes live"

- 🎯 Focus: activate Uber Eats + DoorDash + Skip + push first orders
- 🎯 Pillar mix: Menu Spotlight (35%), Lava Stone (20%), Wellness (15%), Community (15%), Team (10%), Global Kitchen (5%)
- 🎯 First paid test: $300 Meta boost on best-performing organic post (only after 20+ real reviews)
- 🎯 Catering form push — 1 carousel + 1 reel per week
- 🎯 Influencer cadence: 1 Tier-1 visit / week

### Month 3 (Jun 26 → Jul 26) — "Canada Day + family dining"

- 🎯 Focus: own the Canada Day conversation in Brampton food
- 🎯 Flagship campaign: "One Grill. Every Table." Canada Day push (Jul 1)
- 🎯 Ads: $500 Meta on Canada Day + family-platter angle
- 🎯 Guerrilla play: one Gage Park presence (Canada Day)
- 🎯 Catering: office-lunch push for July start
