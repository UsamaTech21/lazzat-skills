## 1. The Five Strategic Priorities (Launch Window — Apr–Jul 2026)

1. **Google reviews from zero → 100+** — QR review card in every order bag; every staff member trained to ask politely.
2. **Build organic social following** — Instagram + TikTok first. No paid until compliance + GMB base is solid.
3. **Activate delivery platforms** — Uber Eats, DoorDash, Skip The Dishes. Paid boost only after organic baseline.
4. **Establish the lava stone story** — every piece of content in launch window touches this pillar at least once per week.
5. **Big Platters = revenue engine** — family platters drive highest AOV. Every campaign has a platter angle.

Every new brief must map to at least one of these five. If it doesn't, question whether to run it.

## 3. Content Strategy Rules

- **One Strength + One Menu Item per post.** Don't mix three angles.
- **Every post maps to exactly one of the 6 pillars.**
- **Platform priority in 2026:** Instagram Reels + TikTok (algorithm distribution beats follower count in first 6 months).
- **Calendar-linked:** every campaign anchored to a cultural calendar event (see `brampton-demographics-intelligence/references/cultural-calendar.md`).
- **Cadence:** 4 posts/week + daily stories minimum.
- **Evergreen vs topical:** 60 / 40.
- **No post without a CTA ending in a question.**

## 7. Competitor Response Playbook

**When a competitor launches something:**
1. Do NOT mention them publicly.
2. Identify the gap they are still missing (use `references/competitor-analysis.md`).
3. Run a Lazzat post within 72 hours that leans into that gap (not reacts to their post).
4. Never run a "look at us vs them" ad. That is below the brand.

**When a customer compares us to a competitor in a review:**
- Thank them. Don't correct them publicly.
- Reply: "Thanks for trying both. We hope Lazzat keeps earning your vote — one bowl at a time."

## 8. Growth Mix — Budget Split (when paid is unlocked)

When Lazzat-Founder unlocks paid budget, the default split is:

| Channel | % of monthly budget | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Meta Ads (IG + FB) | 45% | Biggest Brampton usage, strongest for food |
| Google Search (local) | 20% | Intent-heavy "halal restaurant Brampton" |
| GMB posts (free) | 0% | Weekly, always |
| Delivery platform ads | 20% | Uber Eats / DoorDash boosts |
| Influencer gifting | 10% | Tier 1 Brampton food creators |
| Guerrilla / activations | 5% | One community move per month |

Default budget assumption: **$1,000/mo** after launch; scale to **$3,000/mo** by Q4 2026.

## 9. The "Do-Not-Run" List

Strategies Lazzat does NOT execute:

- ❌ Coupon wars / "$1 bowl" pricing tricks
- ❌ "First 50 customers free" on day 1 (dilutes launch)
- ❌ Paid ads before GMB has 20+ real reviews
- ❌ Influencer day-1 invites (confirmed mistake from prior founders)
- ❌ Email blasts before list is 200+ real opt-ins
- ❌ "Grand reopening" language for a first opening
- ❌ Any campaign that needs a comparison to a competitor to make sense
