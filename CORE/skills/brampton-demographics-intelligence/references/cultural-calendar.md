## Sensitive Windows — Quiet Rules

During any fasting window (Navratri, Ramadan, Lent for some Christians):
- Reduce celebratory meat imagery
- Lead with vegetarian / vegan menu items
- Avoid food-in-daylight reels during Ramadan (evening-only for Iftar content)
- Never post food stories during the exact fasting hours
