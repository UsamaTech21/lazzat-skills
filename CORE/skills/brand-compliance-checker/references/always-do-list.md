---

## 0. Voice (the sound of Lazzat)

**Required on every piece.** Read the copy aloud before you save. If it doesn't sound like a person who owns the place, rewrite.

| Good examples |
|---|
| Short declarative sentences. Fragments OK. |
| "Seventeen sauces. Six continents. One table." |
| "Direct flame. Lava stone. Real fire." |
| "Fresh, not frozen. Prepped daily." |
| "Two grills and shakes stores. Brampton." |

### Preferred vocabulary
- Concrete nouns: skewer, sauce, stone, flame, salt tile, glass, platter, kitchen.
- Named suppliers: **Maple Lodge Farms chicken**.
- Named equipment: Rotoquip + iCombi.
- Named facts: 200g, 17, 13 live, 6, 4, 600M, $6.99, $7.99, $7.49, $3.99.

### Tone by panel type
- **Social captions:** warm, plainspoken, 1–2 sentences, one CTA.
- **Ads (paid):** one fact per line. No filler.
- **Wall panels (in-store):** sentence-or-fragment rhythm; the founder's voice.
- **Blog / long-form:** human voice; still no AI filler; still no comparison to competitors.
- **Press / pitches:** specific, sourced, no superlatives.

## 4. Content Pillar Assignment

**Every piece must map to ONE of the 6 pillars.**

## 6. Menu Accuracy

- Every named dish must be on `menu-v14.md`.
- Every skewer name must be one of: **Chicken Tikka, Lamb Seekh, Paneer, Soya**.
- Every sauce must be one of the **13 live** names; "seventeen sauces from six continents" may be used as a marketing headline only.
- Every salad must be one of the **6 chef-built** salads.
- Every format must be **Cube / Kabab / Wrap / Platter**.
- Every shake price must match the locked point: **$6.99 / $7.99 / $7.49 / $3.99**.
- Every shake ingredient list uses only: real fruit (specific), real milk, real cream, cane sugar, ice.

## 7. Claims Accuracy

- **Salt wall** — state the age as **approximately 600 million years (Khan et al., 2026)**. Never "800M". Never "84 minerals". Never therapeutic.
- **Protein** — state **200g cooked weight**, "honestly weighed, before sauce." Never "raw weight." Same across all four skewers.
- **Chicken** — state **"Maple Lodge Farms chicken."** Never "halal-sourced." Never "hormone-free."
- **Salt wall origin** — "Himalayan salt tiles, from the Khewra region of Pakistan."
- **Shake lowest price** — "$3.99 kids shake" may be stated as a fact. Do not say "lowest in Brampton" unless Lazzat-Compliance has documented the survey.
