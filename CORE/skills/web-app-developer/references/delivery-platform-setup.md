## Platform 1 — Uber Eats Canada

### Registration
- URL: merchants.ubereats.com/ca/en
- Submission: business name, legal entity, HST, hours, menu (upload PDF OR fill manually)
- Approval time: 5–10 business days
- Setup call: Uber reps typically schedule a menu walkthrough

### Menu setup
- Re-enter every item from `menu-v14.md` exactly
- Photos: dark-bg hero shots (from graphic-designer library), WebP/JPG
- Descriptions: 40–80 words each, tone matches Lazzat (no "delicious" / "authentic")
- Categories: Platters, Bowls, Wraps, Kids, Combos, Drinks, Sides, Desserts
- Blocked items per menu-v14: NEVER display

### Commission (as of 2026)
- Uber Eats: 25–30% per order
- Factor this into price decisions (may need 10–15% uplift on Uber menu vs. dine-in; Lazzat-Founder approves)

### Settings
- Delivery radius: 5 km (McVean); 5 km (Clarence)
- Prep time: 20 min (adjust based on kitchen load)
- Availability: mirror store hours
- Images: dark-bg hero; NO stock
- Hide dine-in-only items (e.g., shakes that don't travel well)

## Packaging

Delivery orders need packaging distinct from dine-in:

- Cream paper bag (graphic-designer brief; recyclable)
- Clear seal sticker on top (tamper-evident)
- Sauce in separate portion cups
- Hot items in compostable hot-box
- Thank-you card with Instagram handle + "Leave us a review" message
- Cutlery: compostable — only if customer opts in (default off — Ontario regulation)

## Dispute Protocols

### Customer claims order was wrong
1. Platform first-responder (their CS)
2. If escalated to us: pull POS receipt + kitchen photo (if available)
3. If genuine error: refund + apology DM via platform
4. If pattern emerges: kitchen SOP review with Lazzat-Ads + Lazzat-Culinary

### Rider issue (cold food, late delivery)
- Rider issues are platform-side; we flag to their CS
- Don't accept blame for delivery times beyond our control

### Fraudulent refund requests
- Flag to platform support
- Keep prep photos on file for disputes

---

## Platform Pause Conditions

Pause delivery platforms if:

- Kitchen is over-capacity (> 30 min backlog)
- Key staff absent (kitchen manager + 1 cook minimum)
- Ingredient shortage affecting 20%+ of menu
- POS / tech outage
- Power / internet outage

Always restore within 60 min or communicate expected restoration time on social.
