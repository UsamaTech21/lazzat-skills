# Platform-Specific Rules

> **Rule:** Same story, different shape per platform. Never reuse a caption character-for-character across platforms — the algorithm punishes copy-paste.

---

## 1. Instagram

### Feed
- **Image size:** 1080×1080 (square) or 1080×1350 (portrait)
- **Carousel:** up to 10 slides; best 5–7
- **Caption:** Max 2,200 chars; hook in first 125 chars (before "…more")
- **Hashtags:** 3–5 in-caption, never 30
- **Posting tools:** Meta Business Suite scheduler; backup = Later or Planoly

### Reels
- **Aspect:** 9:16 vertical only
- **Length:** 15–60 sec (sweet spot 20–45 sec)
- **Hook:** first 2 seconds = visual action + on-screen text
- **Subtitles:** always on
- **Audio:** trending ONLY if copyright-safe; original Lazzat sound otherwise
- **Cover:** custom cover image, branded, legible on 3×5 grid
- **Remix:** keep remixes ON (encourages UGC)

### Stories
- **Aspect:** 9:16
- **Story set:** 4–6 frames is sweet spot
- **Stickers:** use at least one interactive sticker (poll, Q&A, location, music)
- **Highlights:** organize into Highlights → "Menu", "Lava Stone", "Team", "Community", "Catering"

### Lives
- **Window:** weekday 13:00 (lunch) or 19:30 (dinner)
- **Length:** 10–20 min launch window; 15 min default otherwise
- **Script:** 3-beat — hook (1 min) → craft moment (10 min) → CTA (1 min)
- **Co-host:** Lazzat-Social + Lazzat-Culinary OR Lazzat-Social + Lazzat-Standards OR Lazzat-Social + customer feature

### What IG punishes
- Link in caption (put in bio)
- Copy-pasted hashtag clusters
- Posting and then editing within 30 min (reach drop)
- Going live without enough followers announcement
- Deleting posts (algorithm penalty)

## 2. TikTok

### Video rules
- **Aspect:** 9:16
- **Length:** 15–60 sec best; up to 3 min allowed but save for Story/brand film
- **Hook:** first 2 sec visual + text overlay
- **Subtitles:** always on, styled (TikTok auto-captions → restyle)
- **Audio:** only sounds marked commercial-safe OR our own original audio
- **Trending use:** only if audio is copyright-safe for businesses

### Caption
- **Length:** 150–2,200 chars; TikTok rewards longer captions with better ranking in 2026
- **Hashtags:** 3–5, mix of brand + broad + niche
- **CTA:** ask a question → comments

### Posting
- **Time:** 18:00–22:00 weekdays for Brampton audience
- **Cross-post:** Reel → TikTok with a re-caption and different audio (avoid watermarks)

### What TikTok punishes
- Instagram watermark on upload
- Copy-pasted captions from IG
- Deleting posts
- Commenting on own videos from new account (bot signal)

---

## 3. Facebook

### Feed
- **Aspect:** 1200×628 (landscape) or 1080×1080 (square)
- **Caption:** First 200 chars show, rest truncated — hook early
- **Best formats for Brampton audience:** carousel + video
- **Groups:** cross-post to Brampton Food Lovers, Brampton Eats, local community groups (with group rules respected)

### Posting
- **Time:** 12:00–14:00 and 18:00–20:00
- **Boost:** only after organic performance proven (min 500 organic reach)

### What FB punishes
- Link bait ("You won't believe…")
- Vague CTAs
- Clickbait headlines
- Repetitive content

---

## 4. YouTube Shorts

### Rules
- **Aspect:** 9:16
- **Length:** 15–60 sec
- **Cross-post Reels** (after 1-day delay to avoid IG algorithm hit)
- **Title:** SEO-optimized ("Best Halal Grill Brampton — Lazzat" not "Reel")
- **Description:** include keywords + link to website
- **Tags:** 5–10 relevant tags

### Why we care
- SEO value — YouTube Shorts appear in Google Search results
- AI SEO — Shorts are now indexed by Google AI Overviews

---

## 5. Google Business Profile Posts

### Rules
- **Length:** 150–300 chars
- **Image:** 720×720 min, 16:9 acceptable
- **Frequency:** 1–2 / week per location
- **Types:** Update, Offer, Event (use Event for calendar moments like Canada Day)
- **CTA:** "Order online", "Visit us", "Learn more"

### What GMB punishes
- Keyword stuffing
- Spammy offers
- Duplicate posts across locations

---

## 6. WhatsApp Business (customer-facing)

### Broadcast list rules
- Only contacts who opted in (never scraped)
- Max 1 message / week
- Never send during daytime Ramadan hours

### Reply rules
- < 2 hours during business hours
- Never share customer phone numbers internally

## Cross-Platform No-Go

Never on ANY platform:
- ❌ Say "Pakistani / Desi / Indian / South Asian / Punjabi / Authentic"
- ❌ Say "charcoal / BBQ / tandoor"
- ❌ Speed / freshness / superlative claims without proof
- ❌ "Halal-certified" (use "Maple Lodge Farms chicken")
- ❌ JazakAllah / Inshallah / religious phrases in marketing copy
- ❌ Off-v14 menu items
- ❌ Unapproved colors or fonts
- ❌ Publish numbers without Lazzat-Founder
- ❌ Delete negative reviews / comments
- ❌ Engage in competitor naming
