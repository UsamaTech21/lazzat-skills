---

## Daily Stories (separate from the 7)

Minimum 3 story frames per day. Ideal 5–7. Types to rotate:

- Prep BTS (morning)
- "Today's bowl" drop (noon)
- Customer moment with consent (afternoon)
- Reel teaser / behind-the-shot (evening)
- Day-close thanks + tomorrow teaser (night)

Engagement tools: polls, Q&A boxes, location stickers, countdown, music (copyright-safe), "add yours".

---

## Time-of-Day Logic (Brampton audience)

- **12:00 lunch feed posts** — Downtown + McVean audience on break; food photos perform
- **18:00 early-evening stories** — post-work commute, highest story view rate
- **19:00 reels** — dinner-adjacent, highest algorithm window for food Reels in Canada
- **11:00 Saturday stories** — weekend-morning lifestyle moment
- **7:00 Sunday morning** — quiet window; avoid for feed; use for scheduled reel upload

Avoid posting: 2–6 AM ET (low reach, damages algorithm).
