# The 10 Repeatable Reel Formats

> **Rule:** Don't invent new formats every week. Use the 10 below. Only propose a new format with Lazzat-Social + Lazzat-Brand sign-off.

---

## Format 1 — Lava Stone Sear Close-Up

**Length:** 9–15 seconds · **Pillar:** 1 (Craft & Ingredients) · **Use:** Awareness + ads

```
HOOK (0–2s): Extreme close-up — lava stone glowing radiant orange.
             TEXT: "No charcoal."
SCENE 1 (2–6s): Raw chicken cube drops onto stone. First sizzle.
             SOUND: Real sizzle, no music overlay
SCENE 2 (6–10s): Char marks form (slow-mo 60fps).
             TEXT: "Just stone. Just flame."
CTA (10–15s): Hero bowl appears.
             TEXT: "Lazzat Grill & Shakes — Brampton."
             LOGO burn-in
```

**Caption first line:** "We replaced charcoal with something better."

## Format 2 — Bowl-Builder POV

```

             TEXT: "Build it yours."

             TEXT: "Base."
SCENE 2 (8–16s): Protein selected — chef tongs it from lava stone.
             TEXT: "Protein. 200 grams. Every time."
SCENE 3 (16–24s): Sauce drizzle — multiple sauces poured.
             TEXT: "One of 13 sauces."
CTA (24–30s): Finished bowl on cream linen.
             TEXT: "Lazzat Brampton. McVean. Clarence."
```

**Caption first line:** "Base. Protein. Sauce. Done."

## Format 4 — 13 Sauces Lineup

**Length:** 15–30 seconds · **Pillar:** 1 · **Use:** Pillar anchor + ad variant

```
HOOK (0–2s): Overhead shot — 13 sauce cups lined up, each labeled.
             TEXT: "Thirteen."
SCENE 1 (2–20s): Each sauce named with quick cut (≈1.5 sec each).
             TEXT: sauce name each cut (e.g., "Lazzat Green", "Charred Tomato", "Mint Yogurt")
SCENE 2 (20–27s): Single bowl, multi-sauce drizzle.
             TEXT: "One for every table."
CTA (27–30s): Logo burn-in.
             TEXT: "See the menu."
```

**Caption first line:** "Thirteen sauces. One grill. Every table."

**Note:** Use only real menu sauces from `menu-v14.md`. No invented sauces.

## Format 5 — Team Story (2-line intro)

**Length:** 30–45 seconds · **Pillar:** 3 · **Use:** Human story

```
HOOK (0–3s): Tight portrait of team member (clean uniform, half-smile).
             TEXT: "Meet [Name]. [Role]."
SCENE 1 (3–15s): Team member in kitchen action (cutting, plating, grilling).
             VOICE: 1 quote from their verified bio (2 sentences max)
SCENE 2 (15–30s): They look to camera, say ONE line about why Lazzat.
             VOICE: "[Specific sentence — pre-approved]"
SCENE 3 (30–40s): Wide shot of team member + another team member laughing.
             TEXT: "[Name] — [Role] at Lazzat."
CTA (40–45s): Logo + tagline.
             TEXT: "One grill. Every story."
```

**Caption first line:** "[Name] runs [specific station] at Lazzat."

**Every line must verify against `team-bios.md`.** No invented quotes.

## Format 10 — Testimonial (customer quote)

**Length:** 15–30 seconds · **Pillar:** 6 · **Use:** Social proof

```
HOOK (0–2s): Smiling guest at table, food in background.
             TEXT: "What Brampton is saying."
SCENE 1 (2–15s): Guest speaks — one quote (with their consent, on camera).
             SUBTITLE: quote burn-in
SCENE 2 (15–25s): Dish close-up (the dish they're talking about).
             TEXT: "[Dish name]."
CTA (25–30s): Logo + "See the menu."
```

**Caption first line:** "[Guest first name]: '[short quote].'"

**Rules:**
- Written consent on file (release form)
- No paid reviews unless disclosed per ASC Canada rules
- No edits that change the guest's meaning
- Real quote only — never scripted
