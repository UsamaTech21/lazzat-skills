# Video Hook Templates

> **Rule:** The first 2 seconds decide the life of a video. If the hook fails, nothing after matters.

---

## The Hook Principles

1. **Verb or noun — never "We" or "Our" first.**
2. **Visual hook > text hook.** Show a striking action before any overlay.
3. **Sound hook helps:** sizzle, scrape of knife, pour of sauce — sound triggers attention.
4. **Stop-the-scroll specificity:** a number, a contrast, a claim.
5. **Don't telegraph the ending.** Make them stay to find out.

## Anti-Hooks (never start a Lazzat video with these)

- "Hey guys!"
- "Welcome back to our channel"
- "Today we're going to show you…"
- "We're so excited to…"
- "Our journey began…"
- "We are thrilled…"
- "Have you ever wondered…"
- Logo-first openers (3+ seconds of logo animation)
- Text-only openers without a visual
- Openers on a white background

---

## Hook Writing Checklist

Before you hit record / export:

- [ ] First 2 seconds visual is striking without sound
- [ ] First 2 seconds has either action, contrast, number, or a question
- [ ] On-screen text (if used) ≤ 8 words
- [ ] No brand-banned words in the hook
- [ ] Hook aligns with the video's pillar
- [ ] Hook gives a reason to watch the next 5 seconds

---

## Testing Hooks

Before committing to a full edit:

1. Cut 3 hook variants (same video, different openers)
2. Post as stories OR Spark Post A/B test (paid)
3. Measure: 3-second view-through rate
4. Pick the winner → full reel + TikTok + Shorts

Rotate hook style monthly so the feed doesn't feel repetitive.
