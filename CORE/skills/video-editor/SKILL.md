## Non-Negotiable Video Rules

1. **Hook in 0–2 seconds.** One visual action OR one bold line. No intros. No logo-first.
2. **Subtitles always on.** Auto-captions at minimum; styled subtitles preferred. 85% of social video is watched muted.
3. **Vertical-first (9:16).** Also export square (1:1) for feed and 16:9 for YouTube landscape where useful.
4. **Length:** Reels + TikToks 9–60 sec. YouTube Shorts ≤ 60 sec. Brand-film cuts may be longer but 90 sec is the upper limit for anything paid-eligible.
5. **Audio:** trending audio allowed only if copyright-safe for business use. Default to Lazzat original sound OR royalty-free.
6. **Frame rate:** 30 fps for social; 24 fps for cinematic brand film cuts.
7. **Every video ends with a visible wordmark or "Lazzat Grill & Shakes" burn-in lower third in the last 2 seconds.**
8. **Tagline:** Primary tagline appears in brand-story videos. Secondary appears in dish-hero videos. Never in every video.
9. **No un-verified team quotes:** if a team member speaks on camera, their line must align with `team-bios.md`.
10. **No regional framing:** no Pakistan/India/Desi/Punjabi flag imagery or music that explicitly signals region.
11. **Compliance block required** for every published video.

  VISUAL: [exact action]

  VISUAL: [action, angle, lens, lighting]
  VOICE/TEXT: [line]

SCENE 2 (X–Y sec):
  VISUAL: ...
  VOICE/TEXT: ...

SCENE 3 (Y–Z sec):
  VISUAL: ...

## Must-Film Shots (Every Shoot Session)

Lazzat-Video brings back from every session:

1. Close-up of lava stone preheat (radiant glow)
2. Raw protein hitting stone (first sizzle)
3. Char mark forming (slow-mo preferred)
4. Steam rise from just-grilled item
5. Finished plate — 45° angle, dark background
6. Bowl build sequence (base → protein → sauce)
7. Sauce drizzle slow-mo
8. Team in action (uniform clean, professional)
9. Guest interaction (face blurred or consent-given)
10. Storefront exterior (blue hour + daytime)

All raw footage sent to Lazzat-Brand same day. **Never delete anything before review.**

## Subtitle & Caption Burn-In Rules

- **Style:** Inter Bold 36pt, white fill, 2px black outline, 70% opacity text drop
- **Position:** horizontally centered, vertically at 80% of frame (above IG bottom UI)
- **Pacing:** 1–2 lines at a time; never walls of text
- **Reading speed:** ≤ 17 characters per second
- **Verification:** auto-generated captions must be manually corrected — proper nouns (Lazzat-Culinary, Lazzat-Culinary, Brampton, McVean, Clarence) are common mis-transcriptions

## Music Clearance

| Source | Use |
|---|---|
| Meta / TikTok licensed library | Organic social only — NOT paid ads |
| Royalty-free (Artlist, Epidemic Sound, Soundstripe) | Paid ads, website, YouTube |
| Lazzat original sound | Brand film master, signature reels |
| Copyrighted commercial tracks | Never (without written license) |

Never "hope it flies" with copyrighted music — it will get muted 2 weeks in and kill the post.

## Recording Guide (short version — full guide in `references/video-recording-guide.md`)

- Film landscape AND vertical per session
- Dark, matte backgrounds (slate, wood, charcoal wall)
- Warm single-source light — never on-camera flash
- Close to the action — fire details matter
- Audio: external mic where possible; ambient kitchen sound is valuable B-roll
- Minimum 3 usable clips per visit
- Backup raw to external drive before leaving venue
- Hand off to Lazzat-Brand same day
