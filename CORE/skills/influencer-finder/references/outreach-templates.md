## Escalation Reply (if creator post becomes an issue)

If a creator's post includes something problematic (unapproved claim, regional framing, negative experience that's factually inaccurate):

**DO NOT respond publicly.**

**Step 1:** Screenshot immediately. Log in Soha's folder.
**Step 2:** Route to Usama + Sir Waqas (message: "creator [handle] posted [issue] — here's the screenshot — recommendation is [do nothing / DM politely / block]").
**Step 3:** If action warranted, DM privately:

```
Hi [Name] — quick note on the [reel / post]. One small thing: [the issue, stated neutrally]. Totally your voice to express, but wanted to flag for accuracy — [our correct framing]. No expectation to edit; just thought you'd want to know.

— Soha
```

Never escalate publicly. Never block without Usama + Sir Waqas sign-off.
