# Creator Vetting Checklist — 10 Points + Score Sheet

> **Rule:** No creator gets a DM from Soha without this checklist passing. Every check is scored; anyone below 70 is skipped.

---

## The 10 Checks

### 1. Location fit (10 pts)
- 10 = Brampton-based
- 8 = Peel Region (Mississauga, Caledon)
- 6 = GTA (Toronto, Vaughan, Markham, Scarborough, etc.)
- 3 = Ontario, non-GTA
- 0 = Outside Ontario (skip)

### 2. Niche relevance (10 pts)
- 10 = Food (halal, Brampton-focus)
- 8 = Food (broader GTA)
- 6 = Lifestyle with food threaded
- 5 = Fitness / health with food relevance
- 3 = Family / parenting
- 0 = Non-aligned (gaming, gambling, political content)

### 3. Engagement rate (10 pts)
Formula: `(avg likes + avg comments) / followers × 100`
- 10 = ≥ 5%
- 8 = 3–4.9%
- 6 = 2–2.9%
- 4 = 1–1.9%
- 0 = < 1% (likely bot-inflated)

### 4. Audience quality (10 pts)
Spot-check 30 recent engagers for realness indicators:
- 10 = 90%+ look real (profile photos, posts, locality)
- 8 = 70–89%
- 6 = 50–69%
- 0 = < 50% real (skip — bot-padded)

### 5. Content consistency (10 pts)
- 10 = Posted 3+ times in past 30 days
- 8 = Posted 1–2 times in past 30 days
- 5 = Posted in past 60 days
- 0 = No activity in past 60 days (skip)

### 6. Brand safety (10 pts)
Review past 90 days of content for red flags:
- 10 = Clean (no red flags)
- 5 = Minor flags (occasional profanity, heavy drinking content — note but may still fit)
- 0 = Major flags (hate speech, harassment, political polarization, anti-halal rhetoric, etc. — skip)

### 7. Halal alignment (10 pts)
For food creators:
- 10 = ≥ 70% halal coverage
- 8 = Mixed but halal-respectful
- 6 = General food, halal-neutral
- 3 = Mostly non-halal (beef/pork-heavy) — skip if it's their main angle
- 0 = Anti-halal content in feed

### 8. Competitor conflict (10 pts)
- 10 = No direct-competitor paid posts in past 60 days
- 5 = Posted general competitor visits but not paid
- 0 = Has active paid deal with Paramount / Osmow's / Mandi House / equivalent — skip

### 9. Tone fit (10 pts)
Sample 3 recent captions:
- 10 = Specific, confident, honest, descriptive
- 8 = Clean and pleasant, minor fluff
- 5 = Heavy on "OMG DELICIOUS" fluff — off-brand tone
- 3 = Meme-only / hype-only voice
- 0 = Hostile, snarky, or negative tone

### 10. Regional framing risk (10 pts)
- 10 = Uses neutral global framing
- 8 = Occasionally regional but not dominant
- 5 = Frequently uses "Pakistani / Desi / Indian" as their primary framing — high risk of miscasting Lazzat
- 0 = Only uses regional framing — skip

---

## Score Interpretation

| Total | Action |
|---|---|
| 90–100 | Immediate outreach (Tier 1 / 2 priority) |
| 75–89 | Outreach with tailored angle |
| 70–74 | Outreach only if niche gap needs filling |
| 50–69 | Hold; revisit in 3 months if fit improves |
| < 50 | Skip — do not contact |

## Automated Pre-Filters (save manual time)

Use free tools for initial filtering before manual vetting:

- **HypeAuditor / Modash / inBeat free** — engagement & audience quality baseline
- **Instagram native Insights** (creator's own screenshot after DM)
- **Google cache of handle** — surface any controversy quickly
- **Brand safety search** — `[handle] controversy OR cancelled OR racist OR scandal`

If any red flag appears, skip. We never salvage a creator with a mid-scale reputational issue.

---

## Re-Vet Cadence

- Tier 1 working list: re-vet every 30 days
- Tier 2 working list: re-vet every 60 days
- Tier 3 working list: re-vet every 90 days

Creators who drop below 70 on re-vet get paused. Sir Waqas is informed if a partnered creator has a major brand-safety incident.

---

## Stop Conditions

Stop outreach immediately if:

- Creator has posted anti-halal, anti-Muslim, anti-immigration, or hate-adjacent content
- Creator engaged in public dispute with another brand unresolved
- Creator's engagement pattern suddenly jumps 10x (likely bot-boost)
- Creator posted for a direct Brampton competitor in past 30 days
- Creator's audience is < 50% Canadian (for Tier 1)
