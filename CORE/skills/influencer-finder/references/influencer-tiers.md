# Influencer Tier System

> **Rule:** Every creator we contact fits one of these three tiers. Nothing else. No improvisation.

---

## Tier 1 — Brampton Local (0–50K followers)

### Priority
**Highest** — Phase 1 (months 1–3). These creators drive our most authentic early word-of-mouth.

### Profile
- Lives in Brampton OR spends ≥ 50% of content in GTA
- Audience is predominantly (≥ 60%) Canadian, with notable Brampton / Peel concentration
- Food / halal / family / lifestyle / fitness / "where to eat in Brampton" content
- Tone matches ours: specific, confident, honest

### Offer
- Free meal (tasting set, 3–5 dishes)
- Bring a plus-one
- "Kitchen tour" mini-experience — lava stone up close
- NO payment in Phase 1

### Expectation
- Honest review
- Tag @lazzatgrill
- Disclosure: `#hosted` or "comp'd by Lazzat" (per ASC Canada guidelines)
- Publish within 14 days

### Example profiles (starter list — reconfirm before DM)

| Handle type | Platform | Audience |
|---|---|---|
| @brampton.food.diary | IG | Brampton families |
| @halalfoodiegta | IG | Halal-first GTA audience |
| @bramptoneats (category tag search) | IG + TikTok | Local food hunters |
| @brampton.momlife (example persona) | IG | Family audience |
| @gta.gymbowls (example persona) | IG + TikTok | Fitness audience |

(Actual handles confirmed by Lazzat-Social weekly; this is the persona template — update every 30 days.)

### Pipeline target
- Identify 20 candidates monthly
- Vet down to 10
- Outreach 5
- Book 2–3

## Tier 2 — GTA / Toronto (50K–500K followers)

### Priority
**Medium** — Phase 2 onwards (months 4–6+).

### Profile
- Based in Toronto / GTA
- Broader "city food" audience; not only Brampton
- Established food/lifestyle channel with professional production
- Trackable reach (uses IG Insights / TikTok Creator Marketplace / CreatorIQ-equivalent)

### Offer structure
- Comped visit (tasting set + plus-one)
- PLUS paid post fee if Phase 2 paid campaign is approved by Lazzat-Founder
- Typical paid range (Toronto benchmarks):
  - 50–100K: $250–$600 per Reel
  - 100–250K: $600–$1,500 per Reel
  - 250–500K: $1,500–$3,500 per Reel
- Usage rights: 30 days organic + 30 days paid boost (negotiate into deal)

### Expectation
- Branded content tagged via IG Branded Content tool
- Disclosure: `#ad` mandatory when paid; `#sponsored` acceptable
- Creative control: 80% creator / 20% brand (we provide must-say list, not a full script)
- Post within 30 days of visit

### Example profile types
- Toronto food tour creators (50–150K)
- Halal Foodie Toronto-type accounts (100K+)
- Fitness / meal-prep creators with GTA audience (100K+)
- Family vloggers in Peel / Halton (100–300K)

### Pipeline target
- 2 Tier 2 partnerships per quarter in Phase 2
- Scale to 1 per month in Phase 3

## Tier 3 — Lifestyle / Health / Niche Creators

### Priority
**Targeted** — Phase 2–3 (months 4–9). These creators expand the brand beyond food-press audience.

### Profile
- NOT primarily a food creator
- Fitness coach, registered dietitian, running club lead, mom-blogger, sustainability advocate, Brampton community builder
- Audience may be smaller (2K–50K) but ultra-engaged and highly aligned

### Angle fit (pick one per partnership)

| Creator type | Angle |
|---|---|
| Fitness coach | "200g honest protein bowl" — high-protein build |
| Registered dietitian | "dietary-inclusive menu" — vegetarian + vegan + allergen-aware |
| Mom / parenting blogger | "kids menu + family table" — Brampton family spot |
| Sustainability advocate | "lava stone = no charcoal emissions" (if we can substantiate with Lazzat-Founder) |
| Community builder | "every table welcome" — brand-story angle |

### Offer
- Comped meal
- Optional co-created content (recipe-ish collab: they design a bowl, we feature it)
- Honest alignment — we pass if their voice is "promo-y"

### Expectation
- Integration feels natural in their normal content (no forced brand takeover)
- Disclosure per ASC Canada
- We never ask them to contradict their professional stance

## The Decision Matrix

When multiple creators fit the same week, prioritize by this stack:

1. **Brampton local** > GTA > outside GTA
2. **Higher engagement rate** > higher follower count
3. **Relevant niche** > general food
4. **No competitor conflict** > has collaborated with competitors
5. **Brand-safe tone** > broad reach but tone mismatch
6. **Halal-aligned content** > not halal-specific

## Banned Partnership Categories

Do NOT partner with creators who:

- Primarily produce alcohol / bar content
- Have posted anti-halal / anti-immigration / politically divisive content in past 12 months
- Were involved in food-hygiene or ethical call-outs without resolution
- Represent competitor restaurants currently (Paramount / Osmow's / Mandi House paid deals)
- Use regional framing the brand has rejected ("best Pakistani / Desi in Brampton")
- Use hate speech, misogyny, racism, homophobia, ableism — in any post within our review window

Celebrity-tier (1M+) and TV-crossover (news channels, syndicated food shows) considered only with:
- Lazzat-Founder approval

- Signed contract (not DM agreement)

These are out of scope until Lazzat has 12 months of operating history AND proven results on Tier 1 + 2.
