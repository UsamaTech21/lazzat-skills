# Platform Search Guide — How to Find the Right Creators

> **Rule:** Every creator we contact was found and vetted in writing. No blind DMs.

---

## Weekly Sourcing Rhythm (Soha's 90-minute block, every Wednesday)

| Minute block | Platform | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| 0–20 | Instagram | Hashtag + geo-tag sweep |
| 20–35 | Instagram | Competitor-tag audit |
| 35–55 | TikTok | Keyword + For You sweep |
| 55–70 | YouTube | Shorts + long-form Brampton food |
| 70–80 | Google Maps / Yelp | Power-reviewer cross-check |
| 80–90 | Log scores in vetting sheet |

Goal: 20 new candidates / week surface to the vetting sheet.

## TikTok

### Step 1 — Keyword search

In TikTok search, try:

- "Brampton food"
- "Brampton eats"
- "Brampton food tour"
- "best restaurant Brampton"
- "best restaurant Brampton 2026"
- "halal food Brampton"
- "halal grill Brampton"
- "new restaurant Brampton"
- "Peel region food"

### Step 2 — TikTok "Users" tab

For each keyword, click the **Users** tab — this surfaces creators, not just videos.

### Step 3 — Sound analysis

If a trending sound is being used in Brampton-food TikToks, click the sound → see who else used it. Fast way to find the local creator cluster.

### Step 4 — TikTok Creator Marketplace (Phase 2)

Once Lazzat has ad account in place, TCM gives data on:
- Follower demographics by region
- Engagement rate
- Audience age / gender
- Rate guidance

## Google Maps + Yelp (Power-Reviewer Method)

### Step 1
Go to each of Lazzat's competitors on Google Maps. Open the reviews panel.

### Step 2
Sort by "Local Guides" or highest reviewer-level.

### Step 3
Click into top 20 reviewer profiles. Check if they have a public social channel linked in their bio or in their reviews ("Follow me @handle").

### Step 4
Cross-check the handles in IG/TikTok. Many Local Guides double as micro-creators.

## Direct Outreach Starters (by channel)

| Channel | How to first contact |
|---|---|
| Instagram | Follow + engage on 2 posts → DM (see outreach-templates.md) |
| TikTok | Follow → duet-reply-friendly comment → DM |
| YouTube | Subscribe → comment thoughtfully → email via channel "Business Inquiries" |
| Email | If contact email in bio — use email (higher response rate than DM) |

**Never DM without having engaged genuinely first.** Cold DMs hit spam filter.

## Geographic Priority Order

When equal-scoring creators compete for the same week:

1. Lives in Brampton (any neighbourhood)
2. Lives in Mississauga (frequents Brampton)
3. Lives in GTA + has regular Brampton content
4. Lives in GTA, Brampton-neutral
5. Lives in Southern Ontario outside GTA
6. Outside Ontario — Phase 3+ only

## Red Flags to Catch Early

- Bio says "DM for collab" without any organic content — usually bot-inflated
- Recent follower spike (2x in a week) — bot-boost
- Comments are all generic emojis / "Nice post!" spam
- Posts only product shots, no face / no voice — lower trust
- Multiple deleted posts or locked stories — opaque history
- Sudden niche shift (food → crypto → food) — instability

## Outreach Cadence After Sourcing

| Day after sourcing | Action |
|---|---|
| Day 0 | Vetted → shortlist |
| Day 1 | Follow + engage on 2 posts (like + thoughtful comment) |
| Day 2 | DM (cold intro — see templates) |
| Day 5 | Follow-up if no reply |
| Day 10 | Final follow-up or close loop |
| Day 30 | Revisit if no reply — their life may have changed |

Never follow-up 3+ times. Move on.
