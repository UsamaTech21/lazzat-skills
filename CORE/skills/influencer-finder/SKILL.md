## CEO-Locked Rules (non-negotiable)

1. **No influencers on opening day (April 26, 2026).** Confirmed by Sir Waqas. First creator visits start Week 2.
2. **Never invite all influencers at once.** One at a time. Sequenced across weeks.
3. **Week 1 post-launch target:** 2–3 vetted creators visit, honest review, no script control.
4. **Always offer free meal first.** Phase 1 (months 1–3) = no paid posts.
5. **Phase 2 (month 4+) paid deals** require Sir Waqas written approval.
6. **Every creator post disclosure required** per Competition Bureau / ASC Canada guidelines (#ad or #sponsored when paid; #hosted when comped).
7. **Compliance pre-check:** the creative brief ships with a compliance checklist even though we don't control the creator's post. If their output violates compliance in a major way, route to Usama — don't respond publicly.
8. **Never approach celebrity-tier (>1M) in Phase 1.** Brand too new; rate-mismatch.
9. **Never badmouth competitors** in briefs or DMs. The competitor is not the story.
10. **Never make creators sign NDAs on taste.** Honest review only.

## The 10-Point Vetting Checklist (every creator, before outreach)

1. **Location** — based in Brampton OR GTA (≤ 60 min drive)
2. **Niche fit** — food / halal / family / fitness / Brampton-local
3. **Audience quality** — engagement ratio ≥ 3% (likes + comments / followers)
4. **Realness** — followers look like real humans (spot-check 30 in their recent engagers)
5. **Content consistency** — posted in past 30 days
6. **Brand safety** — no alcohol-heavy content, no controversy, no recent call-out
7. **Halal alignment** — ≥ 70% of food coverage is halal or halal-friendly (for food creators)
8. **No competitor conflict** — haven't posted a paid spot for a direct competitor in past 60 days
9. **Tone fit** — their voice isn't "OMG LITERALLY DELICIOUS OBSESSED" (off-brand for us)
10. **Regional framing risk** — don't pick creators who constantly use "Desi restaurant Brampton" / "Pakistani food only" — our brand is global

Full rubric + scoring sheet → `references/vetting-checklist.md`.

## The Visit Brief (given to every creator)

```
LAZZAT CREATOR VISIT BRIEF

Hi [Creator name],

Thanks for saying yes. Here's what to expect.

WHEN: [Date, time window]
WHERE: Lazzat Grill & Shakes, [McVean Dr / Clarence St]
PARKING: [note]
WHO'LL MEET YOU: [Soha / Usama / Team member] — they'll introduce you to the team

WHAT WE'LL SERVE:
- A Tasting Set: [3–5 dishes pulled from menu-v14]
- Includes vegetarian + vegan options so you see the full range
- Our 13 signature sauces

WHAT YOU'LL SEE:
- Lava stone grill up close
- Open kitchen — nothing hidden
- Two locations' difference (if relevant)

WHAT WE ASK:
- Honest review. That's it.
- If you post, tag @lazzatgrill and location
- Use #ad or #hosted in line with your platform's disclosure rules
- Let us know when your post goes live (we'll share/tag back)

WHAT WE DON'T DO:
- Scripted lines
- "Must-say" claims
- "Don't mention this" asks
- Pressure to post

NOTES FOR YOUR CONTENT:
- We're a lava stone grill — please don't call it "charcoal" or "BBQ"; the technique is specific
- We use "halal-sourced" (not "halal certified" — our certification is pending)
- No pork, no beef, no alcohol — this is part of the brand
- If you cover dish names, please match our menu naming

ANY DIETARY NEEDS / PREFERENCES? Reply to this DM and we'll prep ahead.

Looking forward,
Soha
Lazzat Grill & Shakes
```
