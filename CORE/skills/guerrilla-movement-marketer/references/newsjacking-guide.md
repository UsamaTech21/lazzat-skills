# News-Jacking Guide

> **Rule:** News-jacking is opportunistic, time-bound, and risky. Follow this guide or skip the trend.

---

## The 2-to-6-Hour Rule

A trend enters our responsible window:
- **EARLIEST:** 1 hour after it's been in mainstream feeds
- **LATEST:** 6 hours after it became visible
- **After 6 hours:** don't touch. The trend is saturated and we look late.

---

## Classify the Trend (Green / Yellow / Red)

### GREEN — ship if fit
- Food / cooking trends (viral recipe, new ingredient, cooking technique)
- Brampton news (civic, sports, positive community stories)
- Cultural moments with broad appeal (Canada wins a big match, Toronto Raptors run, local weather memes)
- National + Canadian pop culture (Drake drops something in Toronto, new Canadian food brand achievement)
- Seasonal / weather memes (snowstorm, heatwave — if safe angle exists)

### YELLOW — discuss with Usama before shipping
- Celebrity news (general; no scandals)
- Sports outcomes (wins ok; losses risky)
- Viral formats with murky origins (check provenance before adopting)
- Cross-cultural events (verify cultural sensitivity first)
- Trends where the "joke" could misfire

### RED — never news-jack
- Tragedy, bereavement, terror attacks, mass casualty, natural disasters
- Political elections / polarizing policy
- Religious events where commercial tone is disrespectful
- Mental health crises / suicide
- Racial, misogynistic, LGBTQ+ harm narratives
- Another brand's crisis (we don't punch)
- Anti-immigration rhetoric
- War / geopolitical conflict (including Kashmir, Ukraine, Gaza, etc.)

## The 4-Step Response Workflow

### Step 1 — Flag (within 30 min of spotting)

Soha flags in #news-jack-lazzat WhatsApp group with:
- Screenshot of the trending content
- 1-line summary of the trend
- 1-line proposed Lazzat angle
- Classification (Green/Yellow/Red)

### Step 2 — Approve (within 60 min of flag)

Usama replies:
- ✅ Approve + notes
- ⏸ Hold + ask for more detail
- ❌ Pass

Red trends auto-skip. Yellow: Usama may escalate to Sir Waqas (15-min SLA).

### Step 3 — Build (within 2 hours of approval)

Route to correct skill:
- **Image trend** → graphic-designer (Canva asset, 60-min turnaround)
- **Video trend** → video-editor (quick cut, 90-min turnaround)
- **Copy trend** → content-writer / social-media-handler (tweet/caption, 30-min turnaround)
- All must ship with brand-compliance-checker pass

### Step 4 — Publish (organic only)

- Post on Instagram + TikTok + Facebook (where fit)
- **NEVER** boost news-jack posts with paid spend (contextual risk is too high)
- Monitor replies for 4 hours
- Sir Waqas kill-switch: if a news-jack sours, Usama may pull in under 10 min

## What "Tied to Lazzat" Actually Means

The Lazzat angle must be genuine:

- **Craft** — does the trend connect to cooking/grilling/food craft?
- **Community** — does it connect to Brampton?
- **Inclusivity** — does it connect to "every table"?
- **Honesty** — does it connect to "200g honest portions"?
- **Team** — does it connect to our team story?

If we have to stretch the connection, we're forcing it. Skip.

## Approved News-Jack Angles by Trend Type

### Weather
- Snowstorm: "Heat. Real flame. Brampton — we got you." + cozy bowl shot
- Heatwave: "Shakes. Stone-cold." + shake hero
- Rain: "Dine-in. We've got seats." + interior shot

### Sports (Raptors / TFC / Canadian Olympians)
- Pre-game: "Team Brampton meal" — Mega Platter
- Win: "Brampton celebrates. We cater." — catering CTA
- **Never** post during loss / poor performance

### Civic / Brampton news
- Brampton opens a new park: "One more reason Brampton's winning." + lava stone platter
- Brampton Transit announcement: "Takeaway for the ride." + bowl shot
- **Never** political endorsements or criticism

### Food trends
- Viral recipe: show it built on our lava stone with our twist
- New ingredient trend: show how we use it (or why we don't)
- Restaurant chain closes in Brampton: **silence**; never punch

### Pop-culture (Drake, Canadian TV, etc.)
- Only if the reference is respectful and broad-appeal
- Never use full song lyrics or stills (copyright)

---

## Never-Ever News-Jack

Even if a competitor news-jacks, we don't chase:

- Illness / medical emergencies
- Political scandals
- Layoffs / corporate bankruptcies
- Celebrity divorces / relationship drama
- Anything where the joke is at someone's expense
- Rumors not yet confirmed
- International tragedy
- Brampton crime (assault, fatality, etc.)

---

## Publishing Checklist

Before posting any news-jack:

- [ ] Trend classified (Green confirmed; Yellow escalated; Red = skip)
- [ ] Usama approved (within 60 min SLA)
- [ ] Lazzat angle is genuine, not stretched
- [ ] No banned words, no regional framing, no unapproved claim
- [ ] No copyright violations (no song lyrics, no movie stills, no sports logos)
- [ ] Ships organic only, never paid
- [ ] Caption has compliance block
- [ ] Sir Waqas notified (if yellow or major moment)
- [ ] Kill-switch plan agreed (who pulls, when)

## Example Mistakes to Study (what we won't do)

- A restaurant in Brampton jumped on a fatal fire nearby with a "we'll feed firefighters" post — tone-deaf timing, backlash. We would not post for 48 hours.
- A chain posted during a Toronto sports loss trying to "cheer up fans" — came across as opportunistic. We only post on wins.
- A brand used a Diwali trending sound with beef imagery — cultural failure. We never use beef imagery; period. And never pair cultural sound with off-tone food imagery.
