## The Five Lazzat Guerrilla Principles

1. **The flame is the spectacle.** The lava stone grill is the marketing asset. Take it outside the store when permits allow.
2. **Film everything.** Every stunt = Reels + TikTok + Shorts. No stunt goes live without a camera.
3. **Community first, brand second.** The stunt must feel like a gift to Brampton, not a sales pitch.
4. **Zero competitor naming.** Never name a competitor in any stunt, caption, or press release. Ever.
5. **Compliance always.** Guerrilla doesn't mean renegade. Every idea passes brand-compliance-checker.

## Non-Negotiables (Lazzat-Founder-confirmed)

- **No religious iconography** in stunts (no temples, mandirs, mosques, gurdwaras as visual backdrop — respectful cultural moments are framed community-first, not religious-institutional)
- **No public-land stunts without municipal permit** (City of Brampton, Downtown Brampton BIA where applicable)
- **No competitor ambush on competitor property** (public land, public events — OK; competitor parking lot — NOT OK)
- **No hot-sauce-waiver stunt in Phase 1** (approved for Phase 2 with actors on stretchers; not for launch window)
- **No political content** (no government endorsements, no hot-button social issues)
- **No unapproved claims** — even in stunts
- **All stunts signed off by Lazzat-Founder**, filmed under Lazzat-Social, logistics by Lazzat-Ads

## News-Jacking — The 2-to-6-Hour Rule

A trend becomes available to Lazzat when:
- It has appeared in mainstream feeds for ≥ 1 hour (not-yet-saturated)
- It has NO political / religious / bereavement tone
- We can honestly tie it to our brand (craft, community, Brampton, dietary inclusivity)

We have **2–6 hours** to ship a response. After that, the trend looks late.

**Workflow:**

1. Lazzat-Social flags trend in team WhatsApp with screenshot + 1-line angle
2. Lazzat-Brand approves angle (≤ 30 min)
3. Content-writer + video-editor / graphic-designer ship asset (≤ 2 hrs)
4. Publish organic only (never paid on news-jacking)
5. Monitor replies; Lazzat-Founder kill-switch if it sours

**Never news-jack:**
- Tragedy, bereavement, terror attacks, natural disasters
- Political elections / polarizing policy moments
- Religious events where commercial tone would be disrespectful
- Brand crises of other companies (we don't punch)

Full rules → `references/newsjacking-guide.md`.

## The Lazzat Brand Movement

Our movement is not a slogan. It's a belief — expressed as three pillars:

1. **EVERY TABLE.** No one excluded from the grill.
2. **REAL FLAME.** Eco-innovation. Lava stone replaces charcoal. Zero coal ash.
3. **BRAMPTON PROUD.** Local roots, Canadian values, global menu.

### Movement Campaign Pillars (detailed in `references/movement-marketing.md`)

- **One Table Challenge** — UGC-driven: customers share a photo of who they ate with today at Lazzat. Tag 3 people you don't usually eat with. We feature the most diverse table weekly.
- **Real Fire Certificate** — every customer who orders a platter gets a collectible "Certified Lava-Stone Grilled" mini-card. Shareable. Instagrammable. Low-cost. Tracks brand exposure organically.
- **Feed Brampton** — for every Mega Platter catering order during Ramadan, Eid al-Fitr, Eid al-Adha, Diwali, and Thanksgiving, Lazzat donates an Iftar/meal to a Brampton food bank or community fridge. Publicly logged.
- **The Open Kitchen Pledge** — every Friday afternoon, Chef Lazzat-Culinary does a 20-min public Q&A in the kitchen. Film it. Archive it. Turns walk-ins into content.

## Idea-Ranking Rubric (before pitching a stunt)

Score every stunt on 5 axes (10 pts each, 50 total):

1. **Lazzat-brand fit** — does it express the 5 brand promises?
2. **Camera-yield** — can we cut 3+ pieces of content from one day?
3. **Community-first framing** — is it genuinely a gift to Brampton?
4. **Cost efficiency** — expected press/organic reach vs. dollars spent?
5. **Reputational safety** — any risk of miscommunication, insensitivity, or city backlash?

Ship only ideas scoring ≥ 40/50. Hold anything below.
