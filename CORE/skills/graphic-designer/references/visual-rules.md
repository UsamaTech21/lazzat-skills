# Lazzat Visual Rules

> **Rule:** If a design breaks these, it never leaves Canva.

---

## 1. The Palette (Wall Posts v4, confirmed)

### Primary
- **Charcoal** `#1A1A1A` — hero, night, grill mood
- **Warm cream** `#F2EBDD` — inviting, day, catering, family

### Accent
- **Deep red** `#C0392B` — CTA buttons, heat highlights, banner flags
- **Warm gold** `#D4AC0D` — premium anchor, anniversary, script tagline

### Supporting (sparingly — max 10% of a composition)
- **Pure white** `#FFFFFF` — type on charcoal
- **Off-black** `#0D0D0D` — type on cream where #1A1A1A feels flat

### Forbidden
- Any blue, green, purple (unless from an approved food-ingredient photograph — not as brand color)
- Pastels
- Neon
- Gradients except charcoal → black heat gradient (approved)

---

## 2. The Grid

- 12-column grid for every social 1:1 / 4:5 / 9:16 layout
- 96px safe-zone inset on 1080×1080 (about 9% of edge)
- Story + Reel: top 250px + bottom 250px are "UI-reserved" — never place critical headlines or logos there
- Carousel: slide 1 = hook; slides 2–N = body; last slide = CTA

---

## 3. Typography System

### The three voices
| Use | Font | Weight | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| Shout (hero) | Arial Black | 900 | "VOLCANIC STONE. REAL FLAME." |
| Speak (body) | Inter / Arial | 400–500 | "Radiant heat from volcanic rock. No charcoal." |
| Whisper (tagline) | Handwritten script | — | *Uniting Through Flavours* |

### Scale rules
- **Hero** = 2× larger than any other text in the composition
- **Body** ≤ 45% of hero size
- **Whisper** ≤ 30% of hero size

### Never
- More than 2 typefaces per composition (script + Arial Black + Inter is borderline — treat script as a graphic element, not a 3rd font)
- Italicize hero lines
- Underline for emphasis (use color or size)
- All-caps for body (ALL CAPS only for hero + banner flags)

---

## 4. Photography Standards

- **Colour temperature:** warm (3200–3800K) — never cool
- **Contrast:** mid-high; preserve shadow detail (never crush blacks)
- **Saturation:** natural + 5%. Never super-saturate food red.
- **Depth of field:** shallow for hero dishes; wider for lifestyle
- **Composition:** rule of thirds or centered-hero only; no Dutch angles
- **Props:** minimal — the food is the hero. One prop (sauce bowl / linen / hand) per shot maximum.

### What makes a Lazzat-approved food shot
- Char marks clearly visible
- Steam (in-camera or subtle comp)
- Sauce reflection / drizzle detail
- Clean plate edge (no smudges)
- Dark slate / charcoal background OR warm cream flat-lay
- 45° or 3/4 angle
- Warm single-source light (natural preferred)

### What makes a rejected shot
- Food on white sterile plate
- Front-flash (flat shadows)
- Top-down 90° overhead for hero dish (OK for flat-lays only)
- Cool tones / blue shadows
- Visible clutter (extra napkins, water rings, busy background)

---

## 5. Logo Usage

### Clear space
Minimum = **1× the logo height** on all sides. Non-negotiable.

### Minimum size
- Digital: 120 px wide
- Print: 20 mm wide

### Approved lockups
- Wordmark horizontal
- Wordmark stacked
- Icon-only
- Wordmark + tagline (vertically centered)

### Colour variants
- White wordmark on charcoal — primary
- Charcoal wordmark on cream — day variant
- Gold wordmark on charcoal — anniversary / premium only
- Never: red wordmark, outlined wordmark, drop-shadowed wordmark

### Logo sins (auto-reject)
- Stretched / squashed ratio
- Rotated > 15°
- Recolored outside palette
- Placed on busy imagery without a solid backing plate
- Combined with a partner logo without Lazzat-Brand + Lazzat-Founder approval

---

## 6. Iconography

- Line icons preferred (1.5–2px stroke)
- Flat, single-weight, no gradients
- Colour: white on charcoal; charcoal on cream
- Size: 48px minimum on social; 24px minimum in signage

Approved icon families: Feather Icons / Iconoir / Lucide
Custom icons: route via Lazzat-Social for brand-fit review.

---

## 7. Motion (for reel covers + animated social)

- Duration: ≤ 3 seconds for cover reveals
- Easing: ease-out (never linear)
- Transitions: cut / dissolve — NO flashy wipes / 3D flips
- Text animation: fade + 4px rise; never bounce
- Max two elements animate per scene

---

## 8. Composition Rules

- **Every composition has one hero:** dish OR headline OR team member. Not two.
- **The eye travels once:** hero → body → CTA → logo. If it zig-zags, redesign.
- **Headline ≤ 6 words** on social; ≤ 10 on banner.
- **Body ≤ 30 words** on social; ≤ 50 on banner.
- **Negative space is a feature.** 25–40% of the composition stays empty.

---

## 9. Accessibility

- Text on charcoal: use white or gold — contrast ratio ≥ 4.5:1
- Text on cream: use charcoal — contrast ratio ≥ 4.5:1
- Minimum type size on social = 24pt body, 48pt hero
- Never use colour alone to convey information (add icon or label)
- Alt text drafted for every digital asset

---

## 10. Do-Not List

- No charcoal imagery (the cooking method isn't charcoal — don't show coal/ember in marketing visuals)
- No tandoor imagery
- No regional flag imagery (Pakistan / India / Bangladesh flags)
- No religious iconography (mandir / mosque / church / gurdwara) in food-marketing visuals
- No stock photography
- No person-less generic backgrounds (always tie imagery to Brampton / Lazzat / team / real food)
- No 3rd-party branding (Canva stock, Freepik watermarks, memojis, emojis as large graphic elements)
- No price callouts in social creative without Lazzat-Brand + Lazzat-Founder sign-off
- No "Best in Brampton" / "#1" / "Award-winning" claims anywhere visual

## 12. File Naming + Versioning

```
LZT_[type]_[campaign]_[YYYY-MM-DD]_[variant].[ext]

Examples:
LZT_feed_LavaStone_2026-04-26_v1.png
LZT_story_Launch_2026-04-25_v2.mp4
LZT_menuboard_McVean_2026-04-01_v3.pdf
LZT_packaging_takeaway-bag_2026-04-10_v1.ai
```

Versions: v1 = first export, v2 = post-feedback, v3 = final approved.
Never overwrite v1 — keep every version in the project folder.
