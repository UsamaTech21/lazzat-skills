## The Brand-Visual Rules (non-negotiable)

| Element | Rule |
|---|---|
| Primary background — social posts | Charcoal `#1A1A1A` OR warm cream `#F2EBDD` (per Wall Posts v4 palette) |
| Primary accent | Deep red `#C0392B` for CTAs + heat highlights |
| Secondary accent | Warm gold `#D4AC0D` for premium / headline emphasis |
| Brand cream | `#F2EBDD` — warm, inviting, use for lifestyle / catering |
| Brand charcoal | `#1A1A1A` — hero + night mood |
| Headline font | **Arial Black** (fallback: Montserrat Black) — heavy, declarative |
| Body font | Arial / Inter / Calibri — clean, legible |
| Accent script (sparing) | Handwritten style for "Uniting Through Flavours" only |
| Logo | White on dark, charcoal on cream. Never on busy patterns. Min clear space = 1× logo height |
| Photography | Dark background (slate/charcoal) OR warm cream flat-lay. 45° above OR 3/4 hero. Char marks, steam, sauce always visible |
| Never | Gradient backgrounds (except approved charcoal-to-black heat gradient), drop shadows on food, white sterile plates, neon colors, stock photos, emoji-heavy layouts |

Full spec → `references/visual-rules.md`.

## Photography Direction (brief to Lazzat-Video / Lazzat-BrandFilm)

### Hero dish shot
- Angle: 3/4 (front-elevated) OR 45° overhead
- Background: slate / dark wood / charcoal
- Light: warm single-source from left or right; window light preferred
- Must show: char marks, steam (in-camera or subtle comp), sauce reflection, garnish crispness
- Frame: fill 70% of frame with food
- Shutter: freeze steam (1/500s+); or slow-shutter for sear-sizzle motion

### Lifestyle shot
- Angle: 3/4 or POV (over-the-shoulder)
- Setting: warm-lit table, human hand reaching in, cream table linen
- Must show: two dishes minimum, a drink, someone's hand for scale
- Mood: communal, inclusive, unposed

### Team / BTS shot
- Angle: eye-level portrait OR 3/4 action
- Must show: uniform clean, posture professional, kitchen backdrop identifiable
- Names required: verify against `team-bios.md` before publishing

### Storefront exterior
- Time: blue hour (20 min after sunset) OR bright afternoon
- Must show: logo/sign, entry doors, at least one guest entering
- No: empty patio, construction signage, competitor storefronts in frame

### Shots to never deliver
- Stock photography
- Food on white sterile plates
- Over-saturated food (supermarket-flyer look)
- Low-light iPhone-grain shots (except authentic BTS reels)
- Any food dish not on `menu-v14.md`

## Typography Rules

| Use case | Font | Weight | Size guide |
|---|---|---|---|
| Poster headline | Arial Black | 900 | 120–240pt |
| Social headline | Arial Black | 900 | 72–96pt |
| Subhead | Arial / Inter | 700 | 42–56pt |
| Body | Arial / Inter | 400–500 | 24–32pt |
| Caption overlay (reels) | Inter | 600 | 36–48pt |
| Tagline | Handwritten script | — | situational, small, sparing |
| Menu item name | Arial Black | 900 | 28–40pt |
| Menu description | Arial | 400 | 14–18pt |

Line height: 1.2 for headlines, 1.4 for body.
Letter-spacing: 0 for body; +20 for ALL-CAP headlines.
Never: more than 2 typefaces per composition.

---

## Logo Rules (summary)

- Minimum clear space = 1× logo height on all sides
- Minimum size = 120px wide on screen; 20mm on print
- Never stretch, rotate > 15°, drop-shadow, outline, recolor outside brand palette, or place on busy imagery without a solid backing plate
- Favicon: just the icon mark, not the wordmark
- OG image: wordmark centered on charcoal with 2px gold rule line

## Colour Combinations (approved)

| Primary | Accent | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Charcoal `#1A1A1A` + white text + red `#C0392B` CTA | gold `#D4AC0D` accent | Premium, hero, night |
| Cream `#F2EBDD` + charcoal text + red CTA | gold accent | Inviting, daytime, catering |
| Charcoal + gold (no red) | — | Anniversary / premium moments |
| Cream + red | — | Festive / cultural moments |

Disallowed:
- Charcoal + cream as equal 50/50 halves (muddy)
- Any single-design use of > 4 colours
- Neon, pastel, non-brand blues/greens/purples
