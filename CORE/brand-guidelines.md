# Lazzat Brand Guidelines

> **STATUS:** Locked — April 2026 (Wall Posts v4 palette confirmed)
> **Owner:** Lazzat-Brand | **Approver:** Lazzat-Founder
> **Rule:** Any deviation from this file requires Lazzat-Founder written approval.

---

## 1. BRAND ESSENCE

**Name:** Lazzat Grill & Shakes
**Meaning:** *Lazzat* — universal word across South Asia, Middle East, North Africa and parts of Europe meaning "pleasure / taste / delight." Intentionally multicultural. Never positioned as "Urdu word" or "Pakistani origin."

**One-Line Positioning:**
> A global premium fast-casual lava stone grill & shakes brand, where every culture finds a plate.

**Primary Tagline:** *Uniting Through Flavours*
**Secondary Tagline:** *Taste the World. Crafted Fresh. Served Fast.*

Use primary on brand film, in-store walls, long-form. Use secondary on delivery platforms, website headers, short-form social.

## 3. COLOR SYSTEM (FINAL — Wall Posts v4 Palette)

### Primary Palette
| Role | Name | HEX | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary | Lazzat Charcoal | `#1A1A1A` | Backgrounds, type, lava stone moments |
| Cream Base | Warm Cream | `#F5E6D3` | Hero backgrounds, wall panels, packaging |
| Signature Accent | Ember Amber | `#D4A574` | CTAs, highlights, premium cues |

### Supporting Palette
| Role | Name | HEX | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Legacy Accent | Lazzat Red | `#C0392B` | Sparingly — logo legacy, emergencies only |
| Fresh Greens | Herb Green | `#6B8E23` | Salad/veg/vegan call-outs |
| Neutral | Off-White | `#FAFAF7` | Body backgrounds, websites |
| Neutral Dark | Soft Black | `#2C2C2C` | Body type |

### Rules
- **70/20/10 split:** 70% Charcoal + Cream neutrals, 20% Ember Amber accents, 10% everything else.
- Never use pure `#FFFFFF` white or pure `#000000` black — always the warm charcoal + cream.
- Never use Lazzat Red as a primary background. It is a legacy accent only.
- Gold (`#D4AC0D`) from v1 is **deprecated** — replaced by Ember Amber.

## 4. TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary
- **Headlines:** Arial Black or Montserrat Black (weight 900)
- **Subheads:** Arial Bold or Montserrat Bold (weight 700)
- **Body:** Arial Regular or Calibri Regular (weight 400)
- **Accent / Display:** Playfair Display (serif — for brand film lower-thirds and premium print only)

### Rules
- Never mix more than 2 type families in one piece.
- Headlines ALL-CAPS or Sentence Case (never Title Case).
- Body never ALL-CAPS.
- Line-height minimum 1.4× font size for readability.
- Never use script fonts, handwritten fonts, or "ethnic" decorative fonts.

---

## 5. LOGO USAGE

- Primary lockup: wordmark "Lazzat" + tagline under
- Monogram: "L" with lava-stone dot — used only as avatar / favicon
- Clear space: minimum of 1× "L" height on all sides
- Minimum size: 32px digital, 20mm print
- **NEVER:** rotate, distort, add gradients, recolor, outline, add drop-shadow, place on busy imagery without a solid base

---

## 6. PHOTOGRAPHY STYLE

### Shot List Rules
- **Overhead flat-lays** on warm cream surfaces (wooden, stone, or linen)
- **Hero shots** against charcoal or lava stone texture backgrounds
- **Action shots** of lava stone grilling — real flame, real smoke, no CGI
- **People shots** — diverse, multi-generational, multi-ethnic ALWAYS in frame when people are shown

### Lighting
- Warm natural light or golden hour
- No cold blue tones, no neon, no clinical white
- Slight grain acceptable; plastic-smooth retouching forbidden

### Props
- Warm cream linen, aged wood, brass/copper accents, lava stone shards
- NEVER: red-checkered tablecloths, banana leaves, marigold flowers, mirror-work textiles (all region-coded)

## 7. BRAND VOICE & LANGUAGE

### Always Say
- Lava stone grill (never charcoal)
- Crafted (never "made")
- Family-style / share plates (never "party pack")
- Every culture welcome / globally loved
- Halal-sourced (never claim halal-certified until documentation confirmed)
- Cube / Kabab / Wrap / Platter (our four menu formats)
- Fresh prep / daily prep (never "farm-to-table" without proof)

### Never Say
- Pakistani / Desi / Punjabi / Indian / South Asian
- JazakAllah / Inshallah / Mashallah / religious phrases
- "Best in Brampton" / "#1" / "voted best" (unproven superlatives)
- "Authentic" — too regional-coded
- "Fusion" — implies inauthenticity; we are globally inclusive, not fusion
- "Cheap" / "affordable" — use "honest pricing" or "fair value"
- "Served in X minutes" — no speed claims until we measure consistently
- "Halal-certified" — until paperwork confirmed
- "Organic" / "farm-to-table" — until sourcing documented

### Sentence Patterns (templates)
- "At Lazzat, ___ meets ___." (e.g. "tradition meets flame")
- "From our lava stone to your table."
- "One grill. Every culture."
- "Built fresh. Served right."

## 9. BRAND PROMISE (FIVE)

1. **Every plate is crafted, not assembled.**
2. **200 grams of protein, honestly weighed.**
3. **Lava stone grill — zero charcoal, zero compromise.**
4. **Every culture welcome. No pork. No beef. No alcohol.**
5. **Open kitchen. Nothing hidden.**
