## NON-NEGOTIABLES (if you break any of these, rewrite)

1. ❌ Never say: Pakistani, Desi, Punjabi, Indian, South Asian, Eastern, Oriental, Ethnic.
2. ❌ Never say: charcoal grill (use direct flame / live fire / lava stone).
3. ❌ Never say: "halal" on any customer-facing surface (signage, menu, social, film, ads, hashtags, delivery descriptions, SEO meta, schema, alt text). Supplier credential is **Maple Lodge Farms chicken**. See voice-and-claims Section 8.
4. ❌ Never name a competitor in customer copy (Cava, Chipotle, Sweetgreen, Nando's, Booster Juice, McDonald's, Tim Hortons, etc.). See voice-and-claims Section 10.
5. ❌ Never disparage Osmow's or the founders' sister brands. Forward-looking / "twenty restaurants taught us..." framing only. See voice-and-claims Section 9.
6. ❌ Never use AI Tier 1 words (delve, tapestry, realm, landscape, leverage, seamless, vibrant, unparalleled, transformative, etc.) or corporate filler (premium, elevated, curated, artisanal, bespoke). See voice-and-claims Section 15.
7. ❌ Never use: health / therapeutic claims (healthy, immunity, detox, superfood, healing, alkalizing, purifying, negative ions, "84 minerals", "healthier than table salt").
8. ❌ Never use: superlatives without proof ("best", "#1", "voted", "cheapest", "unbeatable").
9. ❌ Never use: speed or absolute-freshness claims ("fastest", "always fresh", "fresh fruit" when frozen).
10. ❌ Never use: "smoothie", "juice" (as category), "organic" (uncertified), "hormone-free" (illegal framing), "allergen-free", "gluten-free" without dedicated zone.
11. ❌ Never reference: menu items outside v14. Region-coded dish names (Tandoori Chicken / Tikka Masala / Biryani / Karahi / Haleem / Nihari / Butter Chicken) are banned. **Exception:** "Chicken Tikka" and "Lamb Seekh" are approved protein-skewer names.
12. ❌ Never use: religious phrases in marketing (JazakAllah, Inshallah, Mashallah).
13. ❌ Never use: palette outside the approved HEX list or fonts outside Arial / Montserrat / Calibri / Playfair.
14. ❌ Never invent: quotes, macros, numbers, dates, customer counts, team member words.
15. ❌ Never fail: the seven tests in voice-and-claims Section 18.
